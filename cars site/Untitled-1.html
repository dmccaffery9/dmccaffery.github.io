In 1672, Ferdinand Verbiest, a member of the Jesuit mission in China, invented the first steam powered car ever. However, it was too small to carry a driver. As the years passed, many vehicles were in vogue for a time, and over the next decades such innovations as hand brakes, multi speed transmissions, and better steering developed.

Car: A road vehicle, typically with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine and able to carry a varying number of people.
